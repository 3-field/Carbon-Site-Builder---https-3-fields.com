THREE FIELDS

THREE FIELDS is a collaboration by three artists across India (Deepa Reddy), South Africa (Samukelisiwe Dube), and the UK (Kaajal Modi) developing an innovative immersive artwork exploring food migrations.

Salt

Salt is a prototype game produced by artist Kaajal Modi for EyeMyth Festival, 2026 as part of THREE FIELDS.


Play Salt

This game is a prototype, please send any feedback to the artist at hello@kaajalmodi.com Based on concepts and themes developed collaboratively in discussion by Kaajal Modi (Leeds, UK), Samukelisiwe Dube (Johannesburg, South Africa), and Deepa Reddy (Pondicherry, India) in 2025, as part of THREE FIELDS. Visuals created using artworks made by all three artists during the residency. Words, narrative design and audio recordings by Kaajal Modi. Sound design and original compositions by Ayahi Atelier (Pondicherry, India), using audio recordings from humans, plants and microbes recorded as part of a residency by Kaajal Modi at Kirkgate Market, Leeds in November 2025. Poetry by Kahlil Gibran, Nayyirah Waheed, Divya Victor and featuring original work by Maitrayee Basu. THREE FIELDS is an international project exploring food migrations, land-medicine-spiritual practices, and Indigenous knowledge through artistic making and listening. It is a co-commission between Abandon Normal Devices, Arts Catalyst, Fak’ugesi, Fast Familiar and Unbox, funded by the British Council’s International Collaboration Grants and supported using public funding by Arts Council England. To find out more about the project, go to 3-fields.com
